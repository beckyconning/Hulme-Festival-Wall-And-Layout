We are planning to resurrect Hulme Festival on Saturday 30th July. We're expecting around 2,000 local people to attend and we hope to create a successful community-supported, free, family event that can grow year after year.

The festival will have live music, workshops, stalls and activities ranging from the Zion Centre's 100 year anniversary street-party to a big-chill out tent area supported by the Big Life Company, and lots of other outdoor celebrations.
